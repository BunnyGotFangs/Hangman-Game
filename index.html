Game Play

1. Each blank line repesents a letter

2. You get sixes (6) misses per round (a head, body, 2 arms and 2 legs)

3. A correct guess does not add a body part; an incorrect adds a body part

4. One you have a create a full body and not guessed the word, your pony runs away and you are left hangin'

5. Correct guesses do not add body parts

6. Keep guessing letters until you either solve the word or your pony runs away.

7. Correct letters guess will replace the blank spaces; incorrect guesses will be listed below the gameboard.
